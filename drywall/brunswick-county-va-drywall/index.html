What causes drywall damage?

Drywall damage can occur due to various reasons, including water leaks, impact, structural settling, poor installation, or natural wear and tear over time.

How can I tell that the drywall needs fixing?

Look for signs such as cracks, holes, water stains, or bulging areas on your drywall. If you notice these issues, it's time to consider drywall repair.

Should I fix drywall issues myself, or should I hire a professional?

It depends on the extent of the damage and your DIY skills. Minor damage can often be repaired by homeowners. However, for extensive damage or uncertainty, hiring a professional like us is recommended.

How can I repair bigger holes in the drywall?

For larger holes, you may need to patch the area with a drywall patch or reinforcement tape, apply drywall mud, sand, and then repaint.

Brunswick County is a United States county located on the southern border of the Commonwealth of Virginia. This rural county is known as one of the claimants to be the namesake of Brunswick stew. Brunswick County was created in 1720 from parts of Prince George, Surry and Isle of Wight counties.
